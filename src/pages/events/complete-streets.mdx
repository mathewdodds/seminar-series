Join us for a interactive seminar on March 31st at 9:30 AM to explore the exciting concept of Complete Streets! Our informative presentation will introduce the principles behind this innovative approach to urban design, followed by a lively panel discussion featuring transportation experts sharing their real-world experiences. And get ready to roll up your sleeves, as our interactive design workshop will give you the chance to create and share your own Complete Street using Street Mix or your own hands! Don't miss this opportunity to learn, connect, and explore the future of urban design.
